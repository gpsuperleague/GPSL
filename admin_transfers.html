Transfer window & engine

Draft auctions & schedule

Open/close the owner transfer market on Transfer window.

Reset draft schedule clears start/finish times and turns player + manager + club draft off. Completed transfers and bids are kept.

Cancel open listings & bids

Soft-cancel only: open market / draft listings → Closed (not completed), active bids & pending direct offers → rejected. Completed sales are never touched. Run patches/admin_cancel_open_transfers.sql once if RPCs are missing.

Club auction

Turn Club auction on above and Save transfer settings to share the draft schedule (7pm UK open, random close). Then seed vacant clubs before owners bid. Requires patches/club_auction.sql.

Run transfer engine

Processes stuck listings and draft threads. Run admin_transferengine_run.sql once if the RPC is missing.

If manager auctions are stuck after the random finish, run patches/manager_draft_settlement_fix.sql once, then Settle manager drafts now.
