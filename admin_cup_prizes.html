Cup prize money

Round fees (R1–SF) are paid to both clubs when a tie is confirmed. The final pays separate Winner and Runner-up amounts. Run competition_cup_prizes_fix.sql and patches/cup_prize_winner_runner_up.sql. Creating a new season auto-copies prize tables — or copy manually below (patches/prize_config_persist_across_seasons.sql).
