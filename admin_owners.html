Owner administration

Add owner

Remove owner from club (short break)

Detaches the owner from their club and national team. Season history, rankings, and archives are kept. They can return later via Link existing login to club.

Archive owner (left GPSL)

Same detach as a break, but marks the owner archived (left or removed). History is retained. Unarchive before linking them to a club again.

Unarchive owner

Moves an archived owner to on break so they can be linked to a club again.

Change owner club

Move an owner who already has a club to a different one. Old club is vacated and its nation released. The new club keeps its existing nation assignment. History is retained.

Add owner for club auction (no club yet)

Creates the login, sets £600m pending balance, and status awaiting_club_auction. They only see awaiting_club.html until the auction assigns a club. Requires owner_onboarding_club_auction.sql and the create-owner-club-auction edge function deployed in Supabase.

Link existing login to club

For owners on break with no club, or first-time assignment to a vacant club.

Set owner tag

Sets the owner's Discord tag in gpsl_owner_registry and, if they have a club, Clubs.owner (shown on fixtures, rankings, and club pages). Requires patches/admin_owner_set_tag.sql.

Update email

Set password (admin)

Sets the login password immediately — for testing or when reset email is not enough. Supabase may reject weak passwords (use at least 8 characters with letters and numbers). Deploy the set-owner-password edge function in Supabase.

Send reset email

Sends Supabase’s reset link — owner chooses their own new password.
