Club auction

You are registered for GPSL but do not have a club yet. The club auction is the first step — you will bid from your starting budget (shown below). When the auction completes, your club is assigned, your balance is set, and the full site opens.

Set your owner tag before bidding (shown as the auction leader and on club pages once you win). It cannot be changed after you save it for the club auction.

Set your match availability and timezone before bidding (used for fixture scheduling once you have a club).
