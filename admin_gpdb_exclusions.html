GPDB season exclusions

← Admin hub · Season Break tool to remove players or nations from GPSL for a season (e.g. misconduct, conflict zones). Excluded players are hidden from GPDB and cannot be signed, drafted, or called up. Excluded nations cannot be claimed and their players are treated as excluded. Requires patches/gpdb_season_exclusions.sql.
