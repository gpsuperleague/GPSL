Transfer market rules

Listing & bidding

  • Runs at least 24 hours, until the later of that or 7pm UK.
  • First bid ≥ market value (Value column).
  • Later bids ≥ highest + ₿500,000 (and still ≥ market value).
  • Direct offers (GPDB → Make Offer) also ≥ market value; seller reviews in Transfer Centre.

When time ends

  • Reserve met → highest bidder wins automatically.
  • Reserve not met (with bids) → seller has 24 hours to accept or reject (Seller review).
  • No bids → listing closes; player stays with seller.

Extra time & clocks

  • Bid in final 2 hours → +1 hour once.
  • Then bid in final 5 minutes → +5 minutes (can repeat).
  • Countdown shows time left, UK end, and your local time. Settlement follows shortly after zero.
