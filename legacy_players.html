Legacy players

Cards removed from pesdb.net but still owned in GPSL.

What is a legacy card?

After each PESDB sync, players who are in GPSL but no longer appear on pesdb.net are marked as legacy. They remain at their current club and can still be picked in your squad and play fixtures.

When their contract runs out

  • Cannot be sold or listed on the transfer market at any time.
  • Not on the expiring-contracts wage bid market — other clubs cannot bid for them.
  • In the final contract year, renew from Squad: one season at a time (not a new 3-year deal). Home-grown ≤23 may keep the same wage on renew.
  • Or choose Expire — release for MV from Squad to drop the player for market value.
  • If the card returns on a future PESDB sync, it becomes a normal GPDB card again (sellable, standard renewals).
