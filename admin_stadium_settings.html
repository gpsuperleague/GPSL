Stadium settings

Stadium attendance & club prestige

Club rank (not owner rank) from rolling league + cup points sets stature. Display fill drifts monthly toward the season target; gate revenue is capped at 100% (display can exceed 100% as cushion). Floor 75%. Two on-target seasons ≈ full gate. Run competition_club_stadium_attendance.sql then stadium_attendance_v2.sql, then Recompute club rankings.

Fill limits

Min fill — lowest stadium fill % allowed (gate money and display). Rolling seasons — how many finished seasons feed prestige rank. Cap and cushion are set below under Drift, cushion & performance bands.

Club tier cutoffs (prestige rank)

Maps prestige rank to big, medium, or low club tier (drives expectations and manager lift). Clubs ranked ≤ big cutoff are big; ≤ medium cutoff are medium; the rest are low. Capacity weight and ref seats add a small stadium-size bonus when computing prestige rank.

Expectation vs performance weights

Blend league and cup points into composite scores. Expect weights build the season target; perf weights measure actual results. Typical split is 80% league / 20% cup for each side.

Manager lift (medium/low clubs only)

A strong manager rating raises expected league position for medium and low clubs only (never lowers big-club expectations). Lift starts above Lift from rating, scales to full effect at Max rating ref, and is capped by the max position lift for each tier.

Expected cup points by zone

Cup points baked into season expectation from expected league position and division cup zone (Super8, Plate, Shield, Spoon). League cup default applies when no main-cup zone matches.

Drift, cushion & performance bands (v2)

v2 fill model: display fill drifts monthly toward a season target. Gate cap is the max % used for gate revenue; Max display allows cushion above that. Monthly drift controls how fast fill moves. Prestige step nudges base fill for top/bottom third of the prestige table. Gain / on-target season is fill added each on-target season. Underperformance bands apply slight / bad / abysmal penalties; gap ratios set when each band triggers. Expansion max cap is the largest stadium capacity eligible for new-build expansion.
