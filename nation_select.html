Nation selection

Owners pick a national team in rank order (rolling points from the last four seasons). Initial selection opens after Season 1; re-selection after each World Cup. Your nation is locked until the next World Cup cycle. Nations greyed out on the grid cannot be selected — the GPDB player pool is too small for a 23-man squad or a GPSL club.
