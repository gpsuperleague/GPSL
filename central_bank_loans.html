League loans

All club loans with the GPSL Central Bank. Every loan runs 20 GPSL months (August–May × two seasons when drawn in August; mid-season draws start the next GPSL month). Interest is fixed when the loan is taken and falls as installments are paid — early settlement at the Service counter clears remaining principal and waives leftover interest. Season accounts only list payments posted for that season.
