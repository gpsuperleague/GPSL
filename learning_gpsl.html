Learning GPSL

A quick guide for new owners — how to navigate the site, take part in auctions and transfers, meet club expectations, and get the most from your inbox.

Navigation

  • Dashboard — shortcuts to the pages you use most.
  • Inbox — all owner notifications (results, deals, fines, nation picks, monthly previews).
  • My Club — squad, finances, matchday, stadium, transfer centre.
  • League / Cups — fixtures, tables, stats, cup brackets.
  • Transfers — player & manager databases, markets, draft auctions.
  • Owners — rankings, challenges, and this guide.

Your club badge appears top-left once you are linked to a club.

Auctions & transfers

  • Transfer window — list players, bid on the market, or accept direct offers via Transfer Centre.
  • Draft auctions — timed player and manager drafts; watch the countdown and bid before random finish.
  • Special auctions — occasional league-run events (cash or player prizes).
  • Contracts — check Expiring contracts and renew or replace before players leave.

Completed signings and sales appear in your Inbox.

Matchday & results

  • Play fixtures during the active GPSL month (see calendar badge in the nav).
  • Submit scores and player stats on Match Day.
  • Your opponent confirms or rejects — you are notified at each step.
  • Cup ties cannot end in a draw; use extra time / penalties if needed.

Club expectations

  • Manager — each manager has a league finish target by rating and division. Miss it and they may be released at season end.
  • Stadium & attendance — maintain strong gate fill on Stadium; underperformance reduces revenue and can trigger penalties.
  • Squad size — keep within squad limits; overflow can trigger automatic fines.
  • Fines — matchday misconduct, illegal actions, and admin penalties are notified in your inbox and posted to finances.

Nation & international

Owners pick a national team in ranking order when the selection window opens. You will receive an inbox message when it is your turn — use Nation selection to claim a nation, then manage squads on National team.

Season rhythm

  • Each real-world month (from Friday 19:00 UK) is one GPSL month of fixtures.
  • At month start you receive a match preview in your inbox (opponents, form, threats).
  • End of season: awards (Golden Boot, Ballon d'Or, etc.), manager retention, and a season overview message.
  • Track owner performance on Owner rankings and Season challenges.

Need help?

Check your Inbox first — most actions link straight to the relevant page. League admins can assist with disputes, fines, and technical issues.
