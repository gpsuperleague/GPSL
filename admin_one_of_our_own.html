One of our Own — homegrown star draw

Nav: Admin → Season Break → One of our Own

Draws each selected club a random free-agent player that is home grown (Nation matches the club) with a base Rating of 79+. The draw acts as a real transfer: the player is signed on a contract, appears in the Transfer Centre, and the club is charged the player's market value (balance may go negative) with the fee paid to the GPSL Central Bank. Each club can receive this only once, ever.
