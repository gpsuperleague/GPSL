Weather & pitch conditions

Home venue uses the club’s continent (Clubs.continent or inferred from Nation). GPSL runs August–May; each continent maps those months to Spring / Summer / Autumn / Winter. Percentages are roll weights for new fixtures (Fine / Rain / Snow and Normal / Dry / Wet). Kit season: Summer = short sleeves (spring & summer); Winter = long sleeves (autumn & winter). Northern Europe defaults include more snow; Southern Europe more fine weather.
