Current balance is your spendable cash: opening balance plus all income and costs posted to date (gates, transfers, prizes, etc.). Used by Transfer Centre and the market.

Predicted end-of-season balance uses your current balance plus pending forecasts in Season accounts (upcoming home gates, stadium maintenance, squad wages where calculable). More lines will be added as those systems post to the ledger.

Season challenges

Same structure as the classic Excel workbook. Posted = total already on your balance (ledger this season); Breakdown = individual posted amounts per type; Running total = season opening + posted lines so far; Pending = known future income/costs not yet posted (upcoming gates, wages, maintenance, etc.). Footer shows projected balance.

Government subsidies

Status from your current squad. Payouts post at season end when all league divisions have played 38/38 matches. HG bands pay separately (Quota ≤5, Flying 6–8, Pride 9+); status shows your highest tier. These also appear under Pending in Season accounts until paid.
