Season opening balance is your GPSL starting budget for the season (before club or player auctions), or your archived closing balance carried over from the previous season. Current balance is your spendable cash: opening balance plus all income and costs posted to date. Click the balance box for the full activity ledger. Used by Transfer Centre and the market.

Predicted end-of-season balance uses your current balance plus pending forecasts in Season accounts (upcoming home gates, stadium maintenance, squad wages where calculable).
