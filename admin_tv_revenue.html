TV revenue

Picks up to 5 TV matches per division per month from a combined pool of league and cup fixtures (not separate quotas). Scoring uses table position, cup round, promotion/relegation battles, and dry spells. Payout: 80% home / 20% away of ₿ per TV match (cup finals are always on TV and split 50/50). Selection runs automatically when a GPSL month locks (for the following month). Manual override below if needed.
