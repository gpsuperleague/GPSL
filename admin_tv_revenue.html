League TV picks are per division/month and spread as evenly as possible across that month’s league matchdays (e.g. 12 → 4 per MD in Aug/May with 3 matchdays; 3 per MD in Sep–Apr with 4). Cup TV has its own ₿ pool and monthly quota (cup finals are always on TV and split 50/50; other cups 80/20). League scoring uses table position / title / promo-relegation weights below. Selection also runs when a GPSL month locks (for the next month).
