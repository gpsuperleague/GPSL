GPSL Fair Play Agreement

By joining GPSL you agree to compete in good faith, respect other owners, follow league rules and admin decisions, and not exploit bugs or share accounts. Match results must be honest. Abuse, harassment, or cheating may result in removal from the league.

Further terms may be published in Discord and on Learning GPSL; continued participation means you accept updates announced by the league.
